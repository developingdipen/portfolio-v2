Liquid State | A Responsive Theme

About

Inspired by HTML5's Solid State Theme with but with a creative twist. I used waves as dividers between the Hero Section and the rest of the Website to create a nautical theme. Furthering the nautical element, the sections get darker as you scroll down, similarly to a body of water getting darker the deeper you travel.
