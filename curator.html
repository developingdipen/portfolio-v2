Who am I?

I am a self-taught Front-End Web Developer with a passion for creative minimalism and simplicity when it comes to effective Web Design/Development.

I am driven by the curiosity of learning, the passion for creating and the desire to master new skills. I sometimes believe that less is more and like to employ this thought in my work.

Aside from Web Development, I am a visual creative that enjoys creating with my hands, be it beautifully plated food, curated photography content or stylish men’s ensembles.

What did I do before Web Development?

I studied Kinesiology and Health, and Psychology at Rutgers University in New Brunswick, NJ. Kinesiology is the study of human movement (think Anatomy and Physiology).

I worked as a Physical Therapy Technician in harmony with Physical Therapists to help provide treatments to 100’s of patients for 10’s of illnesses/injuries.
